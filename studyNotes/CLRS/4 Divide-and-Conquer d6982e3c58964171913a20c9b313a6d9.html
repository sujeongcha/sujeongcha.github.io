4.2 Strassen's algorithm for matrix multiplication

  • SQUARE-MATRIX-MULTIPLY: Θ(n^3) times
  • SQUARE-MATRIX-MULTIPLY-RECURSIVE: 8T(n/2)+Θ(n^2)=Θ(n^3) times
  • Strassen's recursive matrix multiplication: Θ(n^log 7), O(n^2.81)

4.3 The substitution method for solving recurrences

  • 1) Guess the form of the solution
  • 2) Use mathematical induction to find the constants and show that the solution works
  • make a distinction between the base case of the recurrence (n = 1) and the base cases of the inductive proof (n = 2 and n = 3)

4.4 The recursion-tree method for solving recurrences

  • recursion tree: each node represents the cost of a single subproblem
  • floors and ceilings usually do not matter when solving recurrences
